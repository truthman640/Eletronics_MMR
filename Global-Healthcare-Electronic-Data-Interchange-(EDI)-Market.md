D) Overview
Healthcare Electronic Data Interchange refers to the structured transmission of healthcare-related data between organizations electronically. It enhances communication efficiency among healthcare payers, providers, pharmaceutical companies, and other stakeholders. The growing complexity of healthcare regulations and increasing demand for compliance and secure data exchange have made EDI systems indispensable in the healthcare industry.

E) Dynamics
