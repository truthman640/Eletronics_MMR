D) Overview:

Solar backsheets are an essential component of PV modules, serving as the protective outer layer that shields sensitive components from environmental factors such as UV radiation, moisture, and mechanical damage. As solar installations expand globally, the demand for reliable backsheets has escalated, making them critical for module longevity and performance.
