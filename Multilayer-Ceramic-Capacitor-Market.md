D) Overview:
Multilayer ceramic capacitors are a cornerstone of modern electronic circuits, valued for their stability, size efficiency, and wide-ranging applications. As digitalization deepens and electronic content in devices rises, the MLCC market continues to evolve with demand for high-capacitance, thermally stable, and miniaturized components.
