D) Overview
Passenger boarding bridges, essential for facilitating safe and efficient boarding at airports and seaports, are experiencing growing demand globally. These bridges offer a wide range of advantages, including enhanced safety, weather protection for passengers, and better operational efficiency for airlines and airport operators. As global travel increases and infrastructure modernization projects continue, the market for these bridges is expected to expand significantly.

E) Dynamics
The primary drivers for growth in the passenger boarding bridges market include the rising demand for improved airport infrastructure, the need for safety enhancements in passenger boarding, and the increasing adoption of automation in airport operations. Additionally, with more airports upgrading to accommodate larger aircraft and higher passenger volumes, demand for advanced boarding solutions is surging. However, challenges such as high installation costs and technological integration may slow the growth rate in certain regions.
