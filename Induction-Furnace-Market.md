D) Market Overview
Induction furnaces are becoming the backbone of modern metal processing due to their energy efficiency, clean operations, and ability to precisely control melting conditions. These furnaces use electromagnetic induction to heat and melt metals without any direct contact with a flame or burner, making them safer and more eco-friendly compared to traditional furnaces. They are widely adopted in foundries, steel plants, automotive, aerospace, and electrical industries.
