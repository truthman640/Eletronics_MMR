D) Overview: IP Cameras are digital surveillance cameras that use internet protocols to transmit data. They are commonly used in various sectors for surveillance and monitoring purposes. The market has witnessed significant growth due to technological advancements, with industries increasingly relying on IP-based solutions for better security management. These cameras provide features like real-time streaming, remote accessibility, and high-definition footage, which are essential for modern surveillance.

E) Dynamics: The market dynamics are influenced by several factors:

Technological Advancements: The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) into IP cameras is enhancing their capabilities, enabling features like facial recognition, motion detection, and automated alerts.

Growing Security Concerns: Increasing crime rates and the need for enhanced security solutions are driving the demand for reliable and scalable surveillance systems.

Regulatory Compliance: Governments worldwide are implementing stringent security and safety regulations, which are boosting the adoption of surveillance solutions in both private and public sectors.
