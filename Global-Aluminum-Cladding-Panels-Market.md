D) Overview:
Aluminum cladding panels have emerged as a preferred choice for architects, contractors, and property owners due to their durability, lightweight nature, energy efficiency, aesthetic appeal, and sustainability. Their resistance to extreme weather conditions and minimal maintenance needs make them ideal for modern construction needs.

Furthermore, rising awareness about green building materials and increased government initiatives promoting sustainable construction have boosted the demand across both developed and developing economies.
