Market Overview:
Fiber Optic Gyroscopes have emerged as critical components in modern navigation systems, offering high accuracy and durability. These gyroscopes are widely used in aircraft, marine vessels, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and oil & gas equipment to enhance orientation, stability, and motion tracking.

Technological advancements, miniaturization, and increased cost-efficiency are driving broader adoption across both defense and commercial applications. Additionally, the growing prevalence of autonomous systems and smart manufacturing is boosting demand for accurate motion-sensing technologies like FOGs.
