D) Overview
Inspection robots are increasingly being deployed in hazardous, hard-to-reach, and high-precision environments where human intervention is risky or inefficient. From oil pipeline inspections and power plant monitoring to automotive assembly lines and food safety checks, these robots are transforming industrial operations. Equipped with visual, ultrasonic, thermal, and laser scanning technologies, inspection robots deliver unmatched reliability and cost-effectiveness.
