D) Overview
Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are critical components used in the electrical and electronics industry to mechanically support and electrically connect electronic components. The market has been evolving rapidly, with innovations in PCB technologies driving their adoption in a wide range of sectors. From consumer electronics to automotive systems, PCBs enable the functioning of a diverse array of devices, making them a key area of focus in the global electronics industry.

E) Dynamics
The Printed Circuit Boards market is influenced by various factors, including the demand for high-performance and miniaturized electronic devices, the increasing need for more efficient, flexible, and cost-effective electronic components, and the growth of automation in various industries. The rapid development of 5G infrastructure and the automotive industry's shift toward electric vehicles (EVs) are further contributing to the growth of the PCB market. However, supply chain disruptions and rising raw material costs remain challenges for market players.
