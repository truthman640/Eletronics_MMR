D) Overview:
Elastomer bearings serve a vital role in load distribution and vibration dampening in structures. Their increasing use in critical infrastructure applications has elevated their significance in civil engineering and construction sectors.

The post-pandemic resurgence of the construction industry, especially in emerging markets, has bolstered the demand for robust and maintenance-free bearing solutions. Moreover, government investments in transportation networks and urban development projects are fueling market expansion.
