Overview
MCCBs are electrical protection devices designed to protect circuits from overcurrent, short circuits, and other electrical faults. Their use has surged due to the rising complexity of electrical systems across sectors. As safety regulations become more stringent and awareness about electrical safety grows, industries are rapidly adopting MCCBs to minimize risk, reduce downtime, and enhance operational efficiency.
