Overview:

Linear actuators, which convert rotary motion into linear displacement, are core components in industries that require precision and efficiency. Their use spans across automotive systems, medical equipment, construction technology, and energy infrastructure. As industries lean into smart automation, the demand for compact, durable, and intelligent actuators is accelerating.
