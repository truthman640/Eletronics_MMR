D) Overview
Hydraulic Workover Units are portable and highly flexible systems used for maintenance and remedial operations in oil and gas wells. Their capabilities extend to plugging, tubing retrieval, sidetracking, and removing stuck pipe‚Äîwithout the need for a full rig. These units are gaining popularity due to their ability to reduce rig time, enhance safety, and operate efficiently in offshore and remote locations.
